Segment Profiles & Strategies

Power Loyalists (31.2%)

Profile: High frequency, high points, broad categories, active redeemer.

Strategy: Deepen Relationship & Upsell. Launch a VIP tier with early access, cross-sell adjacent premium categories, and introduce referral rewards.

Premium Buyers (27%)

Profile: Low frequency, High basket value (1.5x median), Premium categories.

Strategy: Retention & Churn Prevention. Exclusive product previews, early access to seasonal lines, and premium loyalty benefits.

Regular Shoppers (40.7%)

Profile: Mid frequency, Moderate basket, Consistent, Moderate loyalty.

Strategy: Increase Visit Frequency. Bundle offers, personalized reminders after inactivity, and loyalty upgrade incentives.

Churned (1.1%)

Profile: Very high recency (disengaged), Low frequency, Variable discount.

Strategy: Win-Back Reactivation. Implement a 3-touch reactivation journey (reminder emails, time-limited discount codes, surveys).
